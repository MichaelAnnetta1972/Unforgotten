Frequently Asked Questions

Is Unforgotten free to use?

Yes! Unforgotten offers a free plan with core features including medication tracking, appointments, birthdays, and useful contacts for up to 3 profiles. Premium features like unlimited profiles, notes, to-do lists, and family collaboration are available for $4.99/month or $49.99/year.

Can multiple family members access the same account?

Yes! With Premium, you can invite family members with different access levels. Owners have full control, Admins can manage everything, Helpers can update medications and appointments, and Viewers have read-only access. This makes it easy for the whole family to stay coordinated.

Does Unforgotten work offline?

Yes, Unforgotten stores your data locally so you can access it even without an internet connection. Changes sync automatically when you're back online, so you never have to worry about losing information.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. Unforgotten uses row-level security to protect your data. Each user can only access their own account's information, and all data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We take your family's privacy very seriously.

What devices does Unforgotten support?

Unforgotten is available for iPhone and iPad, requiring iOS 17 or later. The app features adaptive layouts that work beautifully on both devices, with larger touch targets and high-contrast themes designed for accessibility.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your App Store account settings. Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your iPhone or iPad. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period.

Can I export my data?

Yes, you can request an export of all your data at any time. Simply contact us at privacy@unforgottenapp.com and we'll provide your data in a portable format.
